License Academy Process

Each module is the building block of an elegant, technically relevant skills development process to achieve a high level of competency development.

Characterize Job Performance Characteristics

Discover the characteristics that contribute to job performance for the organization.

Define Skills

Critically review the job performance characteristics, to define skills that contribute to high levels of productivity.

Incorporate Human Factors & Ergonomics

Enhance job performance by incorporate human factors and ergonomics.

Develop Skillsets

Characterize skills into skillsets that contribute to job competency.

Train Employee

Provide the employee the opportunity to engage with the work, and learn skills for performing the job role.

License or Certify Employee

Certify employees who have demonstrated the learning for performing the job role.
