Chartered Grant Writer (R) Institute Process

Each module is the building block of an elegant, technically complete grant application kit to achieve a high win probability.

Find Sponsors

Explore the world of grants, and sponsorship, and find stakeholders appropriate for your organizational vision and mission.

Review Guidelines

Critically review the criteria set by the sponsoring organization, to ensure the development of a technically complete application kit.

Research Reviewers

Know the reviewers of the foundation, organization, or sponsor who have been bestowed with the responsibility of advancing the organizational mission by helping develop the finest applicants.

Collaborate

Remedy organizational deficiencies by partnering with industry experts.

Define Problem & Present Solution

Each sponsoring organizaton has a mission to help the world make progress. Define the problem creatively, to advance the cause of the sponsor, and present an elegant solution for the challenge.

Craft Narrative & Assemble Kit

Crafting narrative is like developing the season finale, for delighting the stakeholders with a storyline that builds up the climax, and concludes with a satisfying resolution.
